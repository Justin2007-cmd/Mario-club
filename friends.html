meet mario's friends

here are some of mario's most notable friend's

luigi

mario's younger brother. he is taller and thinner than mario,often depicted as more cautious and timid but ver brave when it counts.hewears a green hat and overalls

princess peach

the ruler of the mushroom kingdom,known for her kindness,grace and leadership.often the domsel in distress but also takes an active role in various adventures.she wears a pink dress and a crown.

bowser

he is large,powerful and fearsome creature resembling a dragon-turtul hybrid.known for his imposing size,spiked shell and fiery breath,bowser's main goal is often to kidnap princess peach and conquer the mushroom kingdom.

toad

A loyal attendant to princess peach and a resident of the mushroom kingdom. known for his mushroom-shaped head and blue vest.he's helpful,brave,and always eager to assist mario on his quests.

yoshi

the ruler of the mushroom kingdom,known for her kindness,grace and leadership.often the domsel in distress but also takes an active role in various adventures.she wears a pink dress and a crown.
